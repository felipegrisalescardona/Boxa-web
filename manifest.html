Boxa Projects

Boxa token is the central part of an ecosystem under which different projects will operate.

Boxa Estate

Real Estate tokenization is still something new to crypto but slowly starting to take off. Boxa Estate will combine crypto technology with the real estate sector thru real estate tokenization, investments, building and managing Boxa holder's Hub properties around the world. All while creating a network of properties for the Boxa community and revolutionizing the real estate business.

Boxa Investment Fund

Crypto investors often make risky investments from either buying new pairs with little-to-no information or buying existing projects with only social media hype to go on. B.I.F is an investment fund focusing on crypto projects, dApps and blockchain technology which will spread investment risk for individuals by using the transaction tax fund to invest in solid, vetted projects.

Boxa Royalty Program

Utility and a strong community is what drives a project and from the beginning this was our main focus. This decentralized royalty program was designed to reward the Boxa community by creating a network of benefits and combining a passive income system with real life utility and everyday needs. Think about it like having an AMEX Centurion in your wallet, this is the feeling we want every Boxa token holder to have.

Benefits

By being a Boxa holder, you are not just gaining access to all these benefits, but also enjoying our passive income system. We want even more rewards for the long term holders, and with that in mind, we are introducing a dividend system where you are rewarded with tokens. When someone buys or sells BOXA token, a percentage of the transaction is deducted and redistributed to every BOXA token holder instantly. The longer you hold, the more tokens you will be rewarded with in your wallet.

What is BOXA ?

BOXA is an ERC-20 token, a decentralized project and a cryptocurrency. Boxa token is the Center of an ecosystem, under which Boxa Estate, Boxa Royalty Program and Boxa Investment Fund will operate. From the beginning, our main goal was to create a cryptocurrency with real life utility where it not only can be used as an investment asset but also provides every holder access to a variety of perks and benefits.

Use Case

The Boxa Platform is the new generation of traveling and investing, a project run by Investors and entrepreneurs around the world with a common goal to create one of the best and much needed cryptocurrencies, by combining real life use case with investment and creating the first decentralized royalty program. BOXA token is a deflationary currency, and we want to make sure it will become the cryptocurrency everyone desires to hold in their wallet.

How Does it Work?

Transaction taxes are accumulating in Boxa marketing wallet.

Funds are redistributed between projects according to community needs.

Profits from Boxa projects are reinvested and used to create a strong price floor by doing buy/backs.
