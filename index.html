BOXA token

A community focused and purpose-built investment token, Boxa token was born from an idea that everyday people need investment help. The objective is to make crypto profitable by undertaking the due diligence and execution of trades for the community. Initially launched on the Ethereum platform, Boxa Token has plans for rapid blockchain and investment expansion.

Investment Fund

Crypto investors often make risky investments from either buying new pairs with little-to-no information or by buying existing projects with only social media hype to go on. B.I.F is an investment fund focusing on crypto projects, dApps and blockchain technology which will spread investment risk for individuals by using the transaction tax fund to invest in solid, vetted projects.

B.I.F Concept

B.I.F. will inherently operate like a standard investment fund, however, what sets it apart is a portion of fund profits will be used to buy-back tokens and create a strong price floor and therefor a slow but continuous raise in the price of our Boxa token. In addition, a percent of every transaction made with Boxa will return to the investors in form of daily dividends.

Our ecosystem

From its inception, Boxa token's goal was to do things different. Starting with a smaller supply and gathering a team of developers and investors to create a thriving ecosystem, while maintaining a safe product to invest in.
Together with our tokenomics and future passive income system, Boxa token aims to become one of the best long-term investment projects. Boxa started as a single crypto token - an Ethereum fund that invest in young and established projects. Boxa is now the central part of an Ecosystem, and our main goal is to have a strong project and to give every Boxa holder a high return on their investment.
