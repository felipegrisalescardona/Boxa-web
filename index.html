Boxa Estate

Boxa Estate will combine crypto technology with the real estate sector through real estate tokenization, investments, building and managing Boxa holder's Hub Properties around the world, creating a network of properties for the Boxa community and revolutionizing the real estate and travel business.

We currently have a handful of properties and benefits and are in discussion to add many more, with no plans to cap the number of locations and perks. Imagine Boxa Estate and Boxa Royalty Program as the travel hub as more and more people move to decentralized platforms.

BOXA Token

BOXA is more than a regular token, the Boxa Platform is the new generation of traveling and investing, a project run by investors and entrepreneurs around the world with a common goal to create one of the best and much needed cryptocurrencies, by combining real life use cases with investment and creating the first decentralized royalty program.

BOXA Investment Fund

Crypto investors often make risky investments from either buying new pairs with little-to-no information or by buying existing projects with only social media hype to go on. B.I.F is an investment fund focusing on crypto projects, dApps and blockchain technology which will spread investment risk for individuals by using the transaction tax fund to invest in solid, vetted projects.

Our ecosystem

From the beginning, our main goal was to create a cryptocurrency with real life utility where it not only can be used as an investment asset but also provides every holder access to a variety of perks and benefits. When traveling, you can book a hotel room or an apartment, or eat at a restaurant and your Boxa investment is there to reward you. By holding a specific amount of tokens in your decentralized wallet it gives you access to all of that simply by choosing one of our partners.
