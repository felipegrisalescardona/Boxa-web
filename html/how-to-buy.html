1. Create a crypto Wallet

Create a Coinbase, TrustWallet or MetaMask wallet using either a desktop computer or an IOS/Android mobile device. This will allow you to buy, sell, send, and recieve ETH or BOXA

2. Send ETH to Your Wallet

You can buy Ethereum (ETH) directly on your wallet or transfer it to your crypto wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc. Make sure you use the ERC-20 network when transfering ETH.

3. Connect your Wallet to Uniswap

Access your wallet to Uniswap by clicking 'Connect to a wallet' and selecting your type of wallet.

4. Swap ETH for BOXA

You can start swapping as soon as you have ETH available! Press 'Select a token' and enter the token address or search for it on the token list. Make sure you set the Slippage to 15-18% and use the correct contract.
